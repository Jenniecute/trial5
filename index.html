Schumann frequency, also known as Schumann resonance, refers to the electromagnetic resonances occurring in the Earth's atmosphere. These resonances are caused by the interaction between the Earth's surface and the ionosphere, which is the uppermost layer of the Earth's atmosphere. However, it's important to note that while the Schumann resonance is a natural and fascinating phenomenon, its direct impact on human health is still a topic of scientific research and debate. The majority of scientific studies suggest that the extremely low-frequency electromagnetic fields associated with Schumann resonances are unlikely to have a significant effect on human physiology.
